What is Strava?

The World's Leading
Fitness Community App

Strava is a fitness tracking platform and social network designed for runners, cyclists, swimmers, and athletes of all kinds. It uses your phone's GPS to record your activities, analyze performance, and connect you with a global community of over 120 million athletes.

For students, Strava offers an easy, engaging way to stay consistent with physical activity — whether you're jogging around campus, cycling to class, or joining a group workout challenge.

How to Use

Get Started in 6 Simple Steps

Follow this quick guide to begin using Strava as your daily fitness companion on campus.

1

Download the App

Install Strava for free on the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). It's compatible with most modern smartphones.

2

Sign Up or Log In

Create an account using your email address or sign in quickly with Google or Facebook. Set your height, weight, and activity preferences.

3

Choose an Activity Type

Tap the orange record button and select your activity — run, walk, cycle, swim, or one of 30+ sport types available.

4

Start & Track Your Workout

Hit Start and let GPS lock onto your position. Strava will track your route, pace, and distance in real time.

5

Save & Review Your Activity

Finish your session and save it. Review your map, splits, heart rate (if using a wearable), and personal records.

6

Connect with the Campus

Search for "TechFit UE" club, follow classmates, and participate in campus-wide fitness challenges to stay motivated.

Benefits

Why Students Use Strava

Strava isn't just a tracker — it's a motivational tool that makes fitness a social, data-driven habit for busy university students.

Real-Time Performance Data

Get instant stats on pace, distance, calories, and heart rate so you always know how hard you're working and how to improve.

Social Accountability

Seeing your friends' activities in the feed keeps you accountable and inspired. Kudos and comments create a positive campus fitness culture.

Goal Setting & Challenges

Monthly distance and activity challenges help you set meaningful goals and build consistent exercise habits throughout the school year.

Training History & Progress

Your complete activity log builds over time, making it easy to spot trends, celebrate milestones, and track long-term fitness progress.

Safe Route Awareness

Strava's route builder and heatmaps help you discover safe, popular paths around campus and the city for your workouts.

Wearable Integration

Connects seamlessly with Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, and other devices so all your health data flows into one unified dashboard.
